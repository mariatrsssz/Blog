Exploring London

London is the capital and largest city of both England and the United Kingdom, with a population of 8,866,180 in 2022. Its wider metropolitan area is the largest in Western Europe, with a population of 14.9 million.

Traditional British food

Food
Added By
Maria Trascau
25 feb 2025

1.Fish and Chips: A classic British dish, fish (usually cod or haddock) is battered and deep-fried, served with chunky fries (chips). It’s often accompanied by mushy peas and tartar sauce.

2.Pie and Mash: A traditional London dish, usually consisting of a meat pie (like steak and kidney) served with mashed potatoes and sometimes topped with a parsley sauce called "liquor."

3.Full English Breakfast: A hearty breakfast that includes eggs, sausages, bacon, black pudding, baked beans, grilled tomatoes, mushrooms, and toast. It’s a popular choice for brunch in London.

Most popular things to do in London

Things to do
Added By
Maria Trascau
25 feb 2025

1.Visit the British Museum: This world-famous museum is home to incredible collections of art and artifacts from around the globe, including the Rosetta Stone and Egyptian mummies. And the best part? It's free to enter!

2.See the Tower of London: This historic castle has served as a royal palace, prison, and treasury. It's home to the Crown Jewels and offers a fascinating look at British history. Be sure to check out the Yeoman Warders (aka Beefeaters) and hear their stories.

3.Take a Ride on the London Eye: For panoramic views of the city, a ride on the London Eye (the giant Ferris wheel on the South Bank) is a must. It offers stunning vistas of landmarks like Big Ben, St. Paul’s Cathedral, and the Shard.

The most popular sport in London

Sport
Added By
Maria Trascau
25 feb 2025

The most popular sport in London is football (soccer). Football has a massive following across the city, with London being home to some of the most famous and successful football clubs in the world. Here are a few of the top clubs based in London:

1. Arsenal FC: Based in North London, Arsenal is one of the top clubs in English football, with a rich history and a loyal fanbase. They play at the Emirates Stadium.

2. Chelsea FC: Located in West London, Chelsea is another highly successful club, known for winning domestic and international titles, including the UEFA Champions League. Their home ground is Stamford Bridge.

3.Tottenham Hotspur: Also based in North London, Tottenham has a strong following and plays at the state-of-the-art Tottenham Hotspur Stadium.
